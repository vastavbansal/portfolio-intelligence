## 6. Top 3 Lessons

1. **Weekend geopolitical reversals produce magnitude shocks.** When a binary peace process is unresolved over a weekend, re-weight scenario probabilities toward the BEARISH_RE_ESCALATION tail (assign 40–50%, not 30%). The Monday cash session amplifies; it does not absorb.

2. **Brent +4%/day → Nifty downside multiplier ~7–10×.** A naive linear assumption (−0.2% Nifty for a +4% Brent move) is empirically wrong by an order of magnitude when paired with a confirming domestic signal. Calibrate `session_direction.change_pct` to −0.8 to −1.5% in these regimes; widen `range_low` to 1.5–2.0% below open.

3. **National-leader austerity messages amplify selloffs.** Pattern candidate `AUSTERITY_AMPLIFIES_GEOPOLITICAL_SELLOFF` — first observed today. When a sitting leader signals conservation/austerity during a stress episode, expect 30–50% additional downside vs base case because the market reads it as official acknowledgement of damage.

**Bonus lesson:** FORECAST_PAT005 (Diplomatic Restart Trigger) is fragile across weekends. Pattern-based predictions whose underlying signal is < 2 weeks old should carry a "regime decay" downgrade of one confidence star until a structural anchor (signed MoU, durable FII reversal) appears.
